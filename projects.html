Citrus Tower

"Get to the top, or die trying"

Solo project currently in development. 24-player Action RPG made in Unity

The goal is to get to the top of a randomly-generated tower in a single life.

At the beginning of the session, there are no enemies aside from bosses.

Other players in the session are visible. You can either work with or against them.

A minute after a player dies, be it at the hands of another player or an enemy or hazard, an enemy spawns where they died.

On top of this, the player who died is brought back to the very first floor and their stats are shuffled

Built in Unity with C#
